About

Jinger Zhang is a business multimedia journalist from China, based in New York. Her work has appeared in The Xylom, Next City, NYCity News Service, Dismal Science, and Times Square Investment Journal.

She has covered the crypto industry, market trends, company mergers and acquisitions, immigration, and community stories across New York City — making complex financial and economic subjects accessible to broad audiences, on air and online.

Bilingual in Mandarin and English, Jinger bridges stories across cultures and communities, with a particular focus on how national economic forces shape everyday life.
